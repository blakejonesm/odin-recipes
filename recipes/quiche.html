Coconut Carrot Quiche

Description

Vegan coconut carrot quiche (no eggs!)
Incredibly flexible dish, the ingredients and spices can be changed out for others according to personal preference

Ingredients

  • 1 shortcrust pastry
  • 1 tablespoon wholegrain mustard
  • 200 ml coconut milk
  • 80 ml warm water
  • 50 g chickpea flour
  • ½ teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon sunflower seeds or cumin seeds
  • 4 to 6 sundried tomatoes
  • a few capers or black olives
  • 200 g shredded carrots (2 medium carrots)

Steps

  1. Put pastry in the oven dish, thinly spread mustard on top.
  2. In a large bowl, mix coconut milk, water and chickpea flour. Add curry, cumin , seeds, cut strips of tomatoes, and capers or olives.
  3. Blend in grated carrots. Pour mixture over pastry.
  4. Cook for about 30min at 180°C
