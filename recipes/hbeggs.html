Hard Boiled Eggs

Description

Instructions to hard boil an egg

Ingredients

  • Saucepan
  • Water
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Add uncooked eggs to saucepan
  2. Submerge eggs under water
  3. Bring eggs and water to boil over stovetop
  4. Boil for 10-12 minutes
  5. Remove eggs, submerge in cold water
  6. Once the eggs have cooled, peel
