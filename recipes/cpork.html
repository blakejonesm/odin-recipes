Caramelised Pork

Description

Vegan Caramelised Pork
Delicious caramelised 'pork' without the guilt! Fake meat can be substituted for seitan or portobello mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 500 g plant-based pork (or seitan or oyster mushrooms)
  • 2 shallots or 1 onion
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • ½ teaspoon crushed chilli (adjust for spice level)
  • 4 spring onions
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 200 ml water
  • 3 spring onions

Steps

  1. Heat up oil in a wok and sauté pork on high heat until golden. Remove pork and keep on the side.
  2. Lower the heat and add shallots, ginger and chilli. Cook for a few minutes on low heat, until softened.
  3. Add sugar, fish sauce, soy sauce and water and mix. Bring to a boil while mixing for the sugar to dissolve.
  4. After 5 minutes, add spring onions.
  5. Put pork back in the wok. Boil on high heat for 8-10min, or until the sauce thickens and pork has a nice shiny coat to it.
