Blueberry Muffins

Description

Light and fluffy blueberry muffins

Ingredients (Makes about 10 muffins)

    Muffins:
  • 240 g plain flour
  • 130 g sugar
  • 8 g baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 300 g plant based yogurt
  • 80 ml neutral oil
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 150 to 200 g blueberries (fresh or frozen)

    • Crumble:
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 25 g raw sugar
  • 25 g margarine

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour and brown sugar.
  2. Add the margarine then knead the mixture between your fingers until you obtain a sandy dough that crumbles.
  3. Place in the refrigerator.
  4. Preheat the oven to 220°C
  5. In a bowl mix the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  6. Add the yogurt, oil and vanilla extract then mix quickly without overworking the dough.
  7. Add the blueberries (keep a small part in order to place them on the surface of the muffins afterwards) then mix gently with the spatula, taking care not to crush the blueberries.
  8. Place paper liners in the indentations of a muffin tin and pour the batter to fill them 3/4 full.
  9. Add the remaining blueberries to the surface of the muffins then sprinkle with the crumble.
  10. Bake for 5 minutes at 220°C then lower the temperature to 180°C and continue cooking for 20 to 30 minutes (time varies depending on the oven, watch closely).
  11. The muffins are ready when they are lightly browned, well puffed, and the blade of a knife comes out dry when you stick it inside the muffins.
  12. If using frozen blueberries, let them thaw slightly before using (otherwise they will release too much water and may alter the texture of the muffins).
